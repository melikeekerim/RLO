Activity: reflect on your experience

Reflect on a situation involving a child or young person who exhibited challenging / aggressive behaviour which you have observed or have been involved with and complete the following cycle below. You are able to save your reflection and print it out (as an attachment to your certificate of completion) at the end of this reusable learning object (RLO) which can be used as evidence for revalidation.

PLAN1

What happened

PLAN2

What were you thinking and feeling?

PLAN3

What was good and bad about the experience?

ACT

What were you thinking and feeling?

ACT2

What was good and bad about the experience?

OBSERVE

What sense can you make out of the situation?

OBSERVE2

What else could you have done?

REFLECT

If it arose again what would you do?
